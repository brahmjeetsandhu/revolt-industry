# Revolt Industry

> **A New Era for Comic Creators**

Revolt Industry is a next-generation digital publishing platform built for creators who want to tell original stories without sacrificing creative freedom, ownership, or fair compensation.

Our mission is to build a sustainable ecosystem where manga artists, comic creators, webcomic authors, and light novel writers can create, publish, grow their audience, and develop their intellectual properties into global entertainment brands.

---

## 🌍 Vision

We believe the next generation of legendary stories shouldn't be limited by outdated publishing models.

Revolt Industry exists to give creators:

- Creative freedom
- Fair revenue sharing
- Intellectual property ownership
- Better working conditions
- Global publishing opportunities
- Long-term creator support

# The Problem We're Solving

Traditional publishing has many challenges that limit creators.

## 💰 Poor Monetisation

Creators spend years building incredible stories while often receiving only a small portion of the value they create.

We believe creators deserve to benefit the most from the success of their work.

---

## 😔 Burnout & Working Conditions

Many creators work under:

- unrealistic deadlines
- unhealthy schedules
- excessive workloads
- constant pressure

Great stories require time, passion, and creativity—not burnout.

---

## 🤝 Lack of Support

Independent creators often have to do everything themselves:

- Drawing
- Writing
- Editing
- Publishing
- Marketing
- Audience growth
- Networking
- Business management

Creating a manga shouldn't mean building an entire publishing company alone.

---

## 🎭 Limited Creative Freedom

Many publishers encourage creators to follow trends rather than originality.

Revolt Industry encourages creators to build the stories they truly want to tell.

---

## 🛡 Intellectual Property

Creators should retain ownership of the worlds and characters they create.

Transparency and creator ownership are fundamental values of Revolt Industry.

---

# Frequently Asked Questions

## Reader

### When will Revolt Industry launch?

The platform is currently under development. Join the waitlist to receive launch updates.

### How will readers support creators?

Readers will be able to directly support creators through subscriptions and platform engagement.

### What makes Revolt Industry different?

Our platform is creator-first, focusing on ownership, fair revenue sharing, sustainable publishing, and long-term intellectual property development.

### Will every comic require payment?

No. Our goal is to provide flexible access while ensuring creators are fairly rewarded.

---

## Creator

### Do I keep the rights to my work?

Yes.

Creators retain ownership of their intellectual property unless alternative agreements are mutually agreed upon.

### How do creators make money?

Revenue comes through platform monetisation, creator revenue sharing, and future expansion opportunities.

### Is Revolt Industry free?

Joining the platform is free.

Additional creator opportunities may become available through partnership programmes.

### Can I receive upfront payments?

Selected creators may receive signing payments to help support development.

### What creators are you looking for?

We're looking for passionate creators producing:

- Manga
- Webcomics
- Comics
- Light Novels
- Original Stories
